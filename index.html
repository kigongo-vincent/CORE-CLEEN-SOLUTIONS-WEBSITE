ABOUT
CORECLEEN

Corecleen aims to build and maintain long-term client relationships, with a competitive and excellent cleaning service. The commitment to excellence and professionalism by our cleaning team is why we have earned the loyalty and trust of our regular customers and are the best choice for new clients.

OUR MISSION

To provide our clients with top-quality professional and friendly cleaning services that supersede their expectations. By making the interests of our clients our number one priority we will earn their trust and loyalty and they will become our lifelong customers

OUR VISION

To continually provide excellent services that will propel us to become a household name within the cleaning industry in Uganda

At CoreCleen, we embody the friendly, professional team that we would choose to hire ourselves and offer our contract services to you. Let us take care of your cleaning needs while you concentrate on running your business.
